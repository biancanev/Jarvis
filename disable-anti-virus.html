Why should I turn off Anti-Virus to install Jarvis?

We get it, you're suspicious. Why would a company who created an A.I. need to turn off anti-virus to install their program. After all, you didn't need to turn off any anti-virus protection to use Siri, Cortana, or Alexa. The answer is simple. Siri, Cortana, or whatever A.I you are using came pre-installed in your device. Therefore, it has already been disabled, installed, then re-enabled before you got your phone.


Why don't other apps and programs need to turn off anti-virus. This is also simply answered: those programs don't overwrite files on your device. If it does, there's a good chance that your device will send you a message saying that it is a potential virus. The next steps will take you through an authentication process where you confirm you want to install the program. You don't necessarily have to turn off anti-virus to install Jarvis, but you have to go through the long authentication process before you can continue, and we don't want you to waste your time on that!
